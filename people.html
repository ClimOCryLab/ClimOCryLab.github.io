Prof. Saurabh Rathore (He/Him)

Assistant Professor

Centre for Atmospheric Sciences, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi

Saurabh Rathore is a physical oceanographer working on the ocean's role in climate. Before joining IIT Delhi he completed his PhD at the Institute for Marine and Antarctic Studies (University of Tasmania), in collaboration with CSIRO and the Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution, followed by postdoctoral research at Sorbonne University (LOCEAN, Paris) and UNSW (Sydney). His work spans ocean warming and heat uptake, sea-level rise, marine heatwaves, ocean circulation, and ocean–climate modelling.
